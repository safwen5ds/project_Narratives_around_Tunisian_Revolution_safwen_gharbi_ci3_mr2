Sidi Bouzid: Training session for 30 young people among university graduates
30/10/2016 15:25, TUNIS/Tunisia

(TAP) - The "Youth on the path of development" project was launched, on Sunday in Sidi Bouzid, at the initiative of the association "culture and development" with a funding from the "US-Middle East Partnership Initiative (MEPI).

The six-week project provides a training session for the benefit of 30 unemployed young graduates from higher education from the governorates of Kasserine, Gafsa and Sidi Bouzid, with 10 young people per governorate.

A series of training sessions on the programme carried out by experts with a support given to these young people after training, during the period of developing the files.

A training session in matters project design according to the characteristics of each region and each project is also planned and the organization of working sessions with representatives of banks and with attendance of the concerned authorities.

The project coordinator Najem Salhi said, in a statement to TAP correspondent in the region, that the choice of this project is part of boosting the role of civil society in addressing the problems and help find solutions related in particular to the deterioration of the economic situation, the proliferation of protests and sit-ins to claim the right to employment, the problem of lack of investment culture and implementation of projects by young people among the university graduates.
